Wayanad is a beautiful hill station located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, lush green forests, tea and coffee plantations, and breathtaking waterfalls. Here's a travel guide to help you plan your trip to Wayanad:
Best time to visit: The best time to visit Wayanad is from October to May, when the weather is pleasant and the monsoon rains have subsided.
How to reach: The nearest airport to Wayanad is Calicut International Airport, which is about 100 km away. You can also reach Wayanad by train or bus from major cities like Bangalore and Kochi.
Places to visit: There are many places to visit in Wayanad, some of the popular ones are:
Chembra Peak: It is the highest peak in Wayanad and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
Edakkal Caves: These are prehistoric caves with rock engravings dating back to 6000 BC.
Banasura Sagar Dam: It is the largest earthen dam in India and a popular spot for boating.
Pookode Lake: This is a freshwater lake surrounded by forests and hills.
Soochipara Waterfalls: It is a three-tiered waterfall located in the dense forests of Wayanad.
Things to do: Apart from sightseeing, there are many things to do in Wayanad, such as:
Trekking: There are many trekking trails in Wayanad, including the trek to Chembra Peak.
Wildlife safari: You can go on a wildlife safari in the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary to spot animals like elephants, tigers, and leopards.Plantation visit: Wayanad is famous for its tea and coffee plantations, and you can visit them to learn about the process of cultivation and production. Ayurvedic spa: Wayanad is known for its Ayurvedic spa treatments, and you can indulge in a relaxing spa session.
Accommodation: There are many accommodation options in Wayanad, ranging from luxury resorts to budget homestays. Some of the popular ones are:
Vythiri Resort
The Windflower Resort and Spa
Wayanad Silverwoods Resort
Hiliya Resort
Green Gates Hotel
Wayanad is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. With its scenic beauty and tranquil atmosphere, it is an ideal getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life.
