Wayanad

Wayanad is a popular tourist destination in Kerala, India, known for its natural beauty and rich cultural ...heritage. The district is home to several attractions that cater to different interests, including wildlife, history, adventure, and relaxation.
Trivandrum

Trivandrum, also known as Thiruvananthapuram, is the capital city of Kerala. The city is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic monuments, beautiful beaches, and scenic backwaters. The city is also known for its delicious cuisine, includes seafood, traditional and fusion food influenced by various cultures.
Thrissur

Thrissur, also known as the cultural capital of Kerala, is a district located in the central part of the state. The district is known for its rich cultural heritage, traditional art forms, and historic monuments.
Kottayam

Kottayam is a district located in the southern part of the Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its beautiful backwaters, serene landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.
Idukki

Idukki is a district located in the high ranges of the Western Ghats in the Indian state of Kerala. The district is known for its beautiful landscapes, lush green forests, and serene backwaters.
Ernakulam

Ernakulam is a bustling district in Kerala with a rich cultural heritage and many tourist attractions.
Alapuzha

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is a district located in the southern part of the Indian state of Kerala. The district is known for its beautiful backwaters, sandy beaches, and traditional houseboats.
